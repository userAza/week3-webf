Rug Cleaning Service

A fast, eco-friendly cleaning service for carpets and rugs with pickup and delivery.

Problem & Target Audience: Rugs can be hard to clean at home. Families, pet owners and offices need reliable cleaning without hassle. This is where we intervene with our specialized service!

Core Features:

  • Online booking system
  • Pickup and delivery scheduling
  • Eco-friendly cleaning methods
  • Price calculator by size
  • Subscription for regular cleaning
  • Ratings and feedback

Dog Walking Service

A friendly and reliable service that helps elderly and disabled people take care of their pets by providing safe and caring dog walking assistance.

Problem & Target Audience: Many elderly and disabled people own dogs but struggle to give them regular walks due to mobility issues or other reasons. This service supports them by ensuring their pets stay active, happy and healthy while giving the owners peace of mind.

Core Features:

  • Caring dog walkers
  • Easy online booking system
  • Subscription plans for regular walks
  • Emergency contact and vet support
  • Extra options, like playtime visits or bathing
  • Ratings and feedback
